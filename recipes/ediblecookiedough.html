Edible Cookie Dough

Did Someone Say "Cookie Dough"?!

Mmmmmmm...cookies -cue Homer Simpson's drooling face- However, before they're even baked, you can find some heavenly deliciousness - the dough! Everyone loves cookie dough, and if you don't then you're wrong. However, raw cookie dough can make you very sick so follow these simple steps to ensure your delicious treat is safe to consume!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Heat treat your flour by cooking in the microwave for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring every 15 seconds.
  2. Beat the sugar and butter with an electric mixewr until creamy. Add in the vanilla extract and salt and beat together.
  3. Add in heat-treated flour, mixing until a crumbly dough forms.
  4. Stir in Milk.
  5. Add chocolate chips.
